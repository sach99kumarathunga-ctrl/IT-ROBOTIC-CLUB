GRADE TECH LEADS
Grade Tech Lead Program

Each grade selects outstanding student leaders who guide and motivate their peers in technology activities, competitions, and projects.

  • Coordinate grade-wise technology activities
  • Support club projects and competitions
  • Help organize workshops and events
  • Encourage participation from classmates
  • Assist beginner students in learning
  • Represent grade during club meetings
  • Lead innovation and challenge teams
Why Become a Tech Lead?
  • 🎯 Develop leadership skills
  • 💪 Gain confidence and responsibility
  • 🤝 Improve teamwork and communication
  • 📁 Build a strong portfolio
  • 🏆 Receive recognition and certificates
  • 🌟 Prepare for future leadership roles
"Great leaders do not wait for opportunities — they create them."
